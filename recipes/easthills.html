Description

Is there anything better than a warm panini on a cold winter day? There is not. Anyone who says otherwise cannot be trusted. Behold one that can be trusted, the delicious, the delightful, the warm, the East Hills.

Ingredients

  • Turkey
  • Sourdough Bread
  • Mayo
  • Bacon
  • Muenster

Process

  1. Slice your sourdough bread to the desired thickness.
  2. Spread mayo on one slice of your sourdough bread.
  3. Add muenster cheese, bacon and turkey to bread.
  4. Spritz both slices of bread with olive oil and place on heated panini press for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Meet your maker!
