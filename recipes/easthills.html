Description

Behold the wonderous deliciousness that is the Turkey Hooha. Crafted from the fires of hell, this spicy little number will make you see visions of grandeur and titilate your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • Turkey
  • Sourdough Bread
  • Chipotle Mayo
  • Sliced Tomato
  • Basil
  • Colby Jack

Process

  1. Slice your sourdough bread to the desired thickness.
  2. Spread chipotle mayo on one slice of your sourdough bread.
  3. Add colby jack cheese, sliced tomato, basil and turkey to bread.
  4. Spritz both slices of bread with olive oil and place on heated panini press for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Meet your maker!
