Description

Is she a bitch sometimes? Absolutely. Then again so is life and this deliciuos panini has life in spades. She wakes up every morning and pisses excellence, not for herself, not for you, not for me, but because she can.

Ingredients

  • Turkey
  • Sourdough Bread
  • Lemon
  • Sliced Tomato
  • Basil
  • Colby Jack

Process

  1. Slice your sourdough bread to the desired thickness.
  2. Spread mayo on one slice of your sourdough bread.
  3. Add colby jack cheese, lemon, sliced tomato, basil and turkey to bread.
  4. Spritz both slices of bread with olive oil and place on heated panini press for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Meet your maker!
